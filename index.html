CAD/CAM are software that use computer-based methods to encode geometrical data in which the processes of design and manufacture of automated machinery are highly integrated. While CAD is mainly used to create designs, CAM is the control of machine tools in the manufacturing of parts. The former helps designers and the latter helps reduce manufacturing costs. Both the software require powerful computers
